I am a singer/songwriter from Pittsburgh, PA...
...I go by DiLisio

Mark Albert Jeffries, aka DiLisio (formerly Fly.), was raised on rock 'n roll. It's so in his bloodstream that, like many music snobs, he developed very distinct tastes early on for what he thought was good and what wasn't. And after 27 years of simply being a fan of The Black Keys and Led Zeppelin, he decided it was time to take whatever songwriting skills he possessed at the time and make a rock album. Not realizing that the genres he thought were "bad" growing up (namely hip-hop) contributed mightily to the creation of his first record. Aging with grace. That is the heart of Jeffries, and his music, in a nutshell. Read more
